# My take on Ricoh GR III HDF

**GRIIIx HDF** — the flaws no review video warned me about: the LCD is awful with polarized sunglasses, and the autofocus is honestly frustrating 👎

![Maiden Lane flowers](/assets/photos/gr-hdf-maiden-lane.jpg)

![Beach scene](/assets/photos/gr-hdf-beach.jpg)

![Portrait with bubbles](/assets/photos/gr-hdf-bubbles.jpg)

BUT, this camera makes portraits look soooo good that continuesly make me forgeting how pain in the butt to use it. When the focus hits, the photos have this really special look to them: soft skin tones, slightly muted colors, just enough depth, and that dreamy glow in the highlights from the highlight diffusion filter. It has a vibe that keeps pulling me back in and makes me want to keep shooting. It is also so pokcetable and easy to carry that bringing it along never feels like a hassle.

So yeah, it has plenty of flaws, specially compared with many modern systems and some smartphone cameras, but I still cannot bring myself to complain too much. Because what it captures never feels like just a photo — it feels like a memory you want to come back to again and again.

P.S. Rumor has it the GRIV is coming soon.
